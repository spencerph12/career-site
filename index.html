Computer Hardware Engineering

What Is It?

A computer hardware engineer designs and creates the schematics of computer hardware and other components in a computer. They test the computer hardware they design. Then analyze the test results and modify the design as needed. Generally computer hardware engineers work in research labs with other computer engineers. Copmuter hardware engineers also have ot work with software engineers to be able to have their hardware to work with modern programs.
