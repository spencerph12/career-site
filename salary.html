Salary

Average salary for a computer hardware engineer is $114,600, only 10% earn less the $66,700 and 10% earning more than $172,630

A couple companies that hire computer hardware engineers are, Dell, INTEL, NVIDIA. Though computer hardware engineers are not as in need as they once were they still have an integral use in companies that produce their own microproccesors and chips.
